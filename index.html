A GENERATION IN FLUX

Flux is the state of continuous motion and change.

As soon-to-be graduates of Web Design & Interactive Media, we hover on the edge of student and professional. The rapidly changing digital landscape means that in order for us to grow, we must adapt to emerging technologies, techniques, and ideas.

This process keeps us perpetually in flux.
